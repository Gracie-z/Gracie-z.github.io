I'm a third-year Statistics PhD student at Stanford University, advised by [Sourav Chatterjee](https://souravchatterjee.su.domains/index.html) and [Amir Dembo](https://adembo.su.domains/). Prior to Stanford, I got my B.S. in Honors Mathematics and Data Science from [NYU Shanghai](https://shanghai.nyu.edu/)/NYU, where I was advised by [Wei Wu](https://sites.google.com/view/weiwu-nyushanghai) and [Chenlin Gu](https://chenlin-gu.github.io/).


My research focuses broadly on probability theory, with particular emphasis on problems arising from statistical physics. My current work explores topics including Yang–Mills theory, random walks, and Liouville quantum gravity.
